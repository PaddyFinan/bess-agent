- **Trend:** **RISING** [upgraded from "stable"]

**Signal:** TTF gas has accelerated upward over the past 30 days (€43.37 → €48.68/MWh). Rising gas costs push evening peak power prices higher as gas-fired plants bid up marginal generation costs.

**Implication:** **BULLISH for battery arbitrage spreads.** Higher gas = wider intraday spreads = stronger revenue outlook. This is a **positive development** for project economics. Monitor for sustained movement above €53.5/MWh, which would signal a significant upside scenario beyond base case.

**Risk flag:** None. Rising gas currently supports revenue; spread compression risk would arise if gas prices *fell* sharply while renewable penetration remained high.
