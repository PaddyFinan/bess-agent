## 5. SPREAD BEHAVIOUR ANOMALY

**Observation:** TTF gas rising (+10.4% over 30d) but spreads compressing (-1.8% day-over-day).  

**Historical correlation:** Rising gas → higher evening peak power → wider spreads.  
**Current behaviour:** **Decoupling** — spreads weakening despite supportive gas fundamentals.

**Possible explanations:**
- Increased solar/wind penetration compressing daytime prices faster than gas lifts evening prices
- Demand-side weakness (industrial slowdown, mild weather)
- Battery oversupply eroding capture rates (more BESS = more arbitrage competition = thinner spreads)

**Risk:** If this decoupling persists, **revenue assumptions built on historical gas-spread correlation will overstate future cashflows.**
