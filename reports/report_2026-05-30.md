### **Impact Assessment:**

**BULLISH for project optionality:**
- Confirms Italian government commitment to 9 GW storage target by 2030
- Provides **exit route** from merchant exposure if spreads compress or DSCR deteriorates
- Second auction (16 GWh) larger than first (10 GWh) — signals acceleration

**NEUTRAL/BEARISH for contracted revenue economics:**
- First auction cleared at €12,959/MWh-year — **below project ancillary revenue assumption of €30,000/MWh-year** (€1.5M ÷ 50 MWh)
- If this project were to enter auction, contracted revenue would likely be **€647,950/year** (50 MWh × €12,959), not €1.5M
- This would crater base case DSCR to **~1.35x** (from 2.15x), removing financing headroom

**Strategic implication:**
- **Merchant strategy remains correct** for this project — contracted revenue (at auction prices) insufficient to support €17.5M debt
- MACSE provides **downside hedge** if merchant revenue collapses, but not a value-accretive alternative at current pricing
- Monitor second auction results (Nov 2026) — if clearing price rises materially (toward €20,000+), contracted strategy becomes attractive
