## 🔴 **CRITICAL REGULATORY NEWS**

### Italy Energy Decree — Carbon Cost Removal from Power Bills

**Source:** Bloomberg, Energy Connects, PV Magazine (Feb 18-25, 2026)  
**Impact:** **BEARISH**

The Italian government has **approved legislation to strip carbon costs from retail electricity bills**, preventing pass-through of emissions charges by thermal generators. This directly impacts day-ahead market pricing and threatens merchant BESS arbitrage margins.

**Key implications:**
- **~10% contraction in average daily spreads** expected starting 2027
- Impact worsens over time as solar penetration increases
- PPA market expected to slow sharply due to heightened regulatory risk perception
- Merchant revenue streams face structural compression

**⚠️ ACTION REQUIRED:** This policy change **requires re-underwriting of base case revenue assumptions** for 2027+ cashflows. The current model may overstate energy arbitrage revenues by approximately 10% from next year onward.
